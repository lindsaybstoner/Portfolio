ABOUT

My career interests are focused around user experience and front-end web development where I can draw upon my experience in technology, human behavior, and design to create seamless and intuitive user interfaces.

In my free time I do yoga, search for the perfect latte, constantly browse Zillow and Hotpads, and listen to podcasts like Reply All (broadly about the internet) and Chatty Broads (for the Bachelor/Bachelorette recaps, which is a major guilty pleasure). In my not quite so free time, I enjoy making food from the New York Times cooking app (though it inevitably takes me 2 hours more than the recipe predicts), walking all around Philly, and cleaning the yoga studio for a discounted membership.
